Welcome to Fresco Bar

Where Swansea
comes alive

Tucked behind the National Waterfront Museum with front-row seats to the marina, Fresco Bar has been a beating heart of Swansea's waterfront for years. We're a café, a bar, an ice-cream parlour, and a restaurant all at once — and we wouldn't have it any other way.

Morning coffee, leisurely lunch, sundowner cocktails, or a full evening out — we've got the food, the drinks, and the atmosphere to match every mood.

Our Story

A place for everyone

Fresco Bar was born out of a love for community, good food, and the incredible setting of Swansea's waterfront. Situated right next to the National Waterfront Museum with uninterrupted views of the marina and the sailing boats beyond, we've built something that feels like a second home.

Whether you're in for a morning coffee and a pastry, a proper sit-down lunch, afternoon ice cream with the family, or evening cocktails to the sound of live music — Fresco adapts to you.

We're proud to work with local producers like Gower Brewery and keep our kitchen making everything fresh and with care.
